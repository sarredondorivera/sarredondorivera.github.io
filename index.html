Sofia Arredondo Rivera

I am an Economist (Pre-Doc) currently working at the Financial Research Unit at the Central Bank of Chile (CBC). Before jumping the CBC I worked for a year and a half at the Ministry of Economics and Industry of Chile. I hold a Bachelor's and a Master's degree in Economics from the University of Chile.

My research interests include Industrial Organization and Public Economics, with a focus on financial markets, education and health economics.
